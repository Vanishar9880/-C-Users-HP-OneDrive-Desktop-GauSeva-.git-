What is the best diet for dairy cows?

A balanced diet includes high-quality fodder, mineral supplements, and plenty of fresh water.

How to improve milk production naturally?

Providing a stress-free environment, proper nutrition, and regular veterinary care can boost milk production.

What are the common diseases in cattle?

Common diseases include mastitis, foot and mouth disease, and brucellosis. Regular vaccinations and hygiene practices can prevent these.

How often should cows be milked?

Cows are typically milked twice a day, but some high-yielding breeds may require milking three times a day.

What is the ideal housing for dairy cows?

Cows should have a clean, dry, and well-ventilated shelter with enough space to move around comfortably.

How to manage cattle during extreme weather?

Provide adequate shade and water during hot weather, and ensure warm shelter and dry bedding during cold weather.
